3-Day Itinerary for Mumbai

Your ultimate guide to exploring the Maximum City

Day 1: Iconic Landmarks and Culture

Morning: Start your day with a visit to the Gateway of India, Mumbai’s most iconic landmark. Take a ferry ride to Elephanta Caves, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Afternoon: Explore the Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya (formerly the Prince of Wales Museum) and stroll around the nearby Kala Ghoda district, known for its art galleries and cafés.

Evening: End the day with a visit to Marine Drive to catch the beautiful sunset. Relax at one of the cafés along the promenade.

Day 2: Markets, Street Food & Shopping

Morning: Start your day with shopping at Colaba Causeway, one of Mumbai’s most famous markets. Pick up souvenirs and local crafts, and don't forget to bargain!

Afternoon: Head to Dhobi Ghat to witness the world’s largest open-air laundry. Then, enjoy some delicious street food at Juhu Beach, where you can try local snacks like Vada Pav and Chaat.

Evening: Visit Chowpatty Beach and enjoy a beautiful sunset. End the evening with a dinner cruise along Mumbai's coastline for stunning views of the city’s skyline.

Day 3: Beaches and Seaside Fun

Morning: Start your day at Juhu Beach, one of Mumbai's most popular beaches. Enjoy a peaceful morning walk by the sea, or take part in some local outdoor activities like flying kites or horse riding.

Afternoon: Head over to Bandra Beach for a more relaxed vibe. You can enjoy the beautiful views of the Bandra-Worli Sea Link, or rent a bike and cycle along the coastline.

Evening: Finish your day at Chowpatty Beach, another iconic beach in Mumbai. It’s perfect for a peaceful evening walk with a beautiful view of the sunset. Grab a plate of local snacks like Pav Bhaji or Sev Puri from the beach vendors.
