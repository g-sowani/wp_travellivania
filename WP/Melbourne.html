3-Day Itinerary for Melbourne

Your ultimate guide to exploring the cultural heart of Australia

Day 1: Discover Melbourne’s Culture

Morning: Begin your Melbourne journey at Federation Square, a modern hub filled with cafes, galleries, and museums. Don’t miss the NGV Australia and the Australian Centre for the Moving Image.

Afternoon: Explore the famous Royal Botanic Gardens, a peaceful retreat perfect for a picnic or a leisurely walk. Afterward, take a ride on the Melbourne Star Observation Wheel for panoramic views of the city.

Evening: End the day with a visit to the vibrant Fitzroy neighborhood. Enjoy dinner at one of its trendy restaurants or cafes, followed by drinks at a rooftop bar.

Day 2: Nature, Wildlife & Adventure

Morning: Head out on a day trip along the stunning Great Ocean Road. Stop at picturesque beaches like Torquay and the famous 12 Apostles.

Afternoon: Visit the Healesville Sanctuary to encounter native Australian wildlife, such as kangaroos, koalas, and platypuses.

Evening: Return to Melbourne and enjoy dinner at one of the city’s acclaimed restaurants. For dessert, head to Chokolait in the city center.

Day 3: Art, Shopping & Melbourne’s Hidden Gems

Morning: Start your day with a visit to the famous Hosier Lane for incredible street art and murals. Then, head to Queen Victoria Market for fresh produce, souvenirs, and local goods.

Afternoon: Spend your afternoon at the National Gallery of Victoria or take a walk along the Yarra River to explore Melbourne's diverse architecture and parks.

Evening: For your last evening, enjoy a sunset cocktail at one of Melbourne’s rooftop bars, such as Rooftop Bar or Siglo, and take in the city’s skyline.
