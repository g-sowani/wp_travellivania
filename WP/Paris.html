3-Day Paris Itinerary

Your Ultimate Travel Guide to the City of Lights

Day 1: Explore Iconic Landmarks

Morning: Start your day at the Eiffel Tower. Arrive early to avoid the crowds and enjoy panoramic views from the observation deck.

Afternoon: Embark on a Seine River Cruise to admire the landmarks such as Notre-Dame Cathedral, Musée d'Orsay, and the Louvre.

Evening: End your day in Montmartre. Enjoy the sunset at Sacré-Cœur Basilica and explore the charming streets.

Day 2: Art, Culture & Shopping

Morning: Visit the world-renowned Louvre Museum to see masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

Afternoon: Walk down the Champs-Élysées, one of the world's most famous avenues, and stop at the Arc de Triomphe.

Evening: Explore the Le Marais district for a stylish shopping spree and enjoy dinner at a trendy bistro.

Explore Shopping Spots

Day 3: Gardens, Markets & Hidden Gems

Morning: Start your day with a peaceful walk through the Luxembourg Gardens, perfect for relaxation and photos.

Afternoon: Explore the Musée d'Orsay, an art museum housed in a former train station. Then head to the Marché des Enfants Rouges, the oldest covered market in Paris.

Evening: End your trip with a visit to Notre-Dame Cathedral and a stroll through the charming Latin Quarter.
